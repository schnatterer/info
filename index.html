About me

Throughout the past three decades, I was active in different fields of IT.

Having grown up in software development, I work different domains and roles such as developer, architect trainer, technical project management and leadership.

With the advent of container technology, DevOps gradually became my focus.

I'm a contributor to many open source projects, publish articles, posts and books regularly and love speaking at conferences.

As technical Lead of the infra and consulting teams at Cloudogu, my daily work is about the Cloud, DevOps, GitOps and Kubernetes, among other things.

In this role, I helped to establish modern ops processes in small and large organizations.

My current focus are our internal Kubernetes and GitOps-based developer platform, as well as development of the GitOps Playground.

Since 2021, I'm on the program advisory board of the Mastering GitOps conference.


I'm also available as an independent consultant.
