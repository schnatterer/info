In recent years, I have helped build modern cloud operations for small and large organizations.
This often involves Kubernetes and GitOps-based operations platforms (Internal Developer Platforms, IDPs).
In 2020, at Cloudogu, we therefore started developing the open source GitOps Playground to deploy and operate standardized IDPs in an automated way.

Since 2021, I have been a member of the program committee of the Mastering GitOps conference.

In 2024, I co-authored the book GitOps.

I am a longtime open source contributor, author and speaker.
